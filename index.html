Our Philosophy

We regularly handle workouts of credits of all sizes and levels of complexity, from single-asset real estate loans and commercial loans to local or regional companies, to multi-bank credits to major national real estate and operating companies. We structure and consummate workouts of all different types, from traditional economic restructurings, to collateral recovery or "liquidation" workouts, to "prepackaged bankruptcy plans.

Southern Cross brings to each workout the experience and resources necessary to:

  • Conduct complete due diligence, both legal and business, in connection with the credit and the collateral
  • Establish a workout strategy, timetable and system of professional accountability
  • Devise the most appropriate legal structure for the workout
  • Negotiate the workout
  • Document and close the workout

Our professionals and law firms are experienced in the creation and use of state-of-the-art strategies and structuring techniques for minimizing the risks of "staged" workouts, adversarial litigation, bankruptcy and lender liability claims, many of which strategies and techniques have become industry standards for workout professionals.
